my story

I started preparing towards my goal of becoming a developer!

I will be a developer with strong backgrounds in design. I have a bachelor’s degree with double major in 'Human Environment & Design' and 'Clothing & Textiles' at the Yonsei University. After graduation I worked as a fashion merchandiser for five years. I am skilled at Adobe Illustrator & Photoshop and have an eye for trends and design.

I will be a developer who really enjoy coding itself. As a merchandiser, one of the biggest role is analyzing numbers. I am an expert in Excel and have developed analytic skills. I was thrilled when I made a working formula and found a better, easier, or faster way to finish a certain task with Excel. Writing new code, solving problems step by step and fixing debugs give me same thrill!

I will be a developer who is a team player. Because of the nature of the design major, I took many classes which required team projects. For example, a team decided one design concept and team members developed each products based on the concept. Also at work, I was a team member for launching a new brand, which required lots of cooperation with other departments. Those experiences enabled me to become a self-motivated team player.
